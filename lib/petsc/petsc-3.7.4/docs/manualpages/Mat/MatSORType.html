MatSORType

 What type of (S)SOR to perform

Synopsis

typedef enum {SOR_FORWARD_SWEEP=1,SOR_BACKWARD_SWEEP=2,SOR_SYMMETRIC_SWEEP=3,
              SOR_LOCAL_FORWARD_SWEEP=4,SOR_LOCAL_BACKWARD_SWEEP=8,

May be bitwise ORd together
