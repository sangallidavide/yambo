DMForestSetBaseDM

 During the pre-setup phase, set the DM that defines the base mesh of a DMForest forest. The forest will be hierarchically refined from the base, and all refinements/coarsenings of the forest will share its base. In general, two forest must share a bse to be comparable, to do things like construct interpolators.

Synopsis

#include "petscdmforest.h" 
#include "petscdm.h" 
PetscErrorCode DMForestSetBaseDM(DM dm, DM base)
Logically collective on dm

Input Parameters

dm - the forest
base - the base DM of the forest
