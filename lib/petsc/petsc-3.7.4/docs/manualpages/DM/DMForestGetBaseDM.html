DMForestGetBaseDM

 Get the base DM of a DMForest forest. The forest will be hierarchically refined from the base, and all refinements/coarsenings of the forest will share its base. In general, two forest must share a bse to be comparable, to do things like construct interpolators.

Synopsis

#include "petscdmforest.h" 
#include "petscdm.h" 
PetscErrorCode DMForestGetBaseDM(DM dm, DM *base)
Not collective

Input Parameter

dm -the forest

Output Parameter

base -the base DM of the forest
